Investment Policy of Magrow Holding Company

* IP Conversion as capital: Co-invest with the private sector that has received rights from research or innovation to further commercialize it.  
* Venture Capital: Co-invest with researchers, professors or students who wish to bring research or innovation results to produce themselves in order to expand in the form of start up.  
* Joint Venture: Jointly invest or invest with the private sector to set up a company to bring knowledge, commercial property, research or innovation from the university to provide production and distribution services.  
* Venture Builder: Jointly invest in the nation by supporting entrepreneurs from the beginning. Finding an idea that can be developed into a real business helps support the business, helps find funding, provides specialized advice, and helps build connections.
